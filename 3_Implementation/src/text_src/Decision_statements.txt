                                        Decision statements- Introduction
                                        
    Decision making statements in a programming language helps the programmer to transfer the control from one part
	to other parts of program. These decision making statements facilitiate the programmer in determining flow of control.
	The process is nothing but execution of one and more instruction sequentially in order in which they come into sight.
	In a real time applications, there are a number of situation where one has to change order of execution of statements
	based on condition.
